Welcome!

Elegant Mail: sending messages to those you love.

Elegant Mail is inspired by a Brazilian tradition. It is called "Correio Elegante" in Portuguese, and it is usually done in the June festivities.

How does Elegant Mail work?

First, think about that person who deserves to hear some love words today. Then choose to send between anonymous or signed messages. Write your message, and share Elegant Mail with everyone!
